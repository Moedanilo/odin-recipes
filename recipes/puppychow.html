Puppy Chow

Description

Ever wonder what would be awesome to make for your slumber parties or snowy day? Puppy Chow is a great option if you are!

I recommend this easy, sweet and tasty recipe. Here are your ingredients!!!

Ingredients
  • Chocalate Chips
  • Peanut Butter
  • Rice Cereal Squares
  • Confectioners sugar
Steps
  1. Melt the chocolate and peanut butter together.
  2. Remove from the heat and stir in the cereal.
  3. Pour the sugar into a large resealable bag.
  4. Add cereal and shake until well-coated.
